What to bring

FRN required.

A Federal Registration Number (FRN) is required for the paperwork. Social Security Numbers are not accepted. An email address is also required by the FCC and for the FRN.

For what to bring to an exam session, including FRN-related information, see ARRL’s guidance. Links on ARRL also help search for other in-person or online exam locations.
