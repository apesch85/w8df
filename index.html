Membership

Join the society.

Membership is open to all persons having an interest in amateur radio. Dues are $20 annually and payable January 1.

For new amateurs, dues are waived for the remainder of the calendar year in which they are first licensed. Members receive the monthly club newsletter, FEEDBACK, electronically or by USPS on request.

Applications may be mailed to SMARS, P.O. Box 934, Battle Creek, MI 49016, brought to a meeting, or submitted electronically.
