Battle Creek, Michigan · Amateur Radio Club

Clear signal. Steady hands. Friendly operators.

SMARS / W8DF is the Battle Creek area home for amateur radio: club meetings, repeaters, local nets, emergency communications, license testing, code practice, swap-shop listings, newsletters, and a practical path for new hams to get on the air.
