Directions

From east or west.

Take I-94 to exit 112 Partello Road. Turn right at the end of the exit. Follow Michigan Avenue into Marshall to Marshall Street, the first traffic light, and turn left heading south. Turn left onto Fair Street and proceed into the fairgrounds.

Vendors will be located in the Commercial Building. Check-in will take place under the awning at the eastern building entrance.
